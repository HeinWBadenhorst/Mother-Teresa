Sample screen capture depicting "Rife Frequency Generator".

  1. This buttons will sort the frequency list in ascending or descending order.
  2. Click on this button to remove any duplicates from frequency list.
  3. Click on this button to reschedule the runtime to 60 minutes.
  4. Select appropriate volume by dragging the volume bar.
  5. "Run" will start the rife session.
